FTO Internship Program

A 2-Month Internship to Kickstart Your Career!

About the Internship

The Federal Tax Ombudsman (FTO) offers a 2-month internship program aimed at providing valuable work experience to students and recent graduates. This internship allows you to gain hands-on experience in various departments of the FTO.

Why Join Our Internship?

Hands-on Experience

Gain real-world experience working alongside professionals in the field of tax administration.

Networking Opportunities

Build connections with industry experts, mentors, and peers that will help you in your career journey.

Certificate of Completion

Earn a prestigious certificate that will enhance your resume and boost your career prospects.
