Special Initiatives

Taxpayer Facilitation Initiative

This initiative aims to improve the taxpayer experience through streamlined processes, reducing the burden of tax compliance.

Read More

The Taxpayer Facilitation Initiative focuses on simplifying the filing process and providing dedicated support to taxpayers. It aims to enhance transparency and reduce compliance costs, making it easier for citizens to fulfill their tax obligations.

Public Awareness Campaign

FTO is working to increase public awareness about tax laws and rights to empower citizens and ensure better tax compliance.

Read More

The Public Awareness Campaign helps educate citizens on the importance of taxes and the role of tax revenues in national development. The campaign includes workshops, seminars, and media outreach to spread information on taxpayer rights and responsibilities.

Digital Tax Services

Enhancing the digital infrastructure to provide online services, making it easier for taxpayers to access services and file taxes electronically.

Read More

This initiative focuses on digitizing the tax services provided by FTO. With the adoption of online platforms, taxpayers can access services like online filing, inquiry submission, and status tracking, making the tax process more efficient and user-friendly.
